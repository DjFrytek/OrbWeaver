Instructions

  • Use WSAD to move the camera
  • Use scroll to zoom camera
  • Use numbers 0-9 and space to spawn obstacles
  • Control their strength with a slider or number key
  • F to spawn Finishline, C to spawn checkpoint
  • Grab nodes to move and change size of obstacles
  • Press DELETE while holding a node to delete the obstacle
  • Hold SHIFT to snap nodes to grid
